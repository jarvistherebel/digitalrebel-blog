Affiliate Marketing

The Affiliate Marketing System That Prints Money

How I built an automated engine for passive income

I don't sell products. I recommend them. And I get paid every time someone buys. Here's the complete system I built to generate passive income through affiliate marketing.

The Business Model

Affiliate marketing is simple:

  1. I review products I use and believe in
  2. Readers click my affiliate links
  3. They buy the product
  4. I earn a commission (15-60%)

The magic? I do the work once (write the article), and it keeps earning for years.

Why AI Tools?

I chose the AI productivity niche because:

  • High growth: AI adoption is exploding
  • High commissions: SaaS products pay 30-60% recurring
  • Evergreen: People always need productivity tools
  • I use them: Authentic recommendations convert better

The System Architecture

1. Content Engine

Every article targets a specific buyer intent:

Review Articles (30%)

Example: "beehiiv Review 2025: Is It Worth It?"

  • Target: People ready to buy
  • Intent: High (they're researching before purchasing)
  • Conversion: 5-10%
  • Commission: $25-50 per sale

Comparison Articles (25%)

Example: "beehiiv vs Substack vs ConvertKit"

  • Target: People deciding between options
  • Intent: High (they're in decision mode)
  • Conversion: 3-7%
  • Commission: Multiple products featured

Roundup Articles (20%)

Example: "10 Best AI Tools for Productivity"

  • Target: Broad audience researching options
  • Intent: Medium (early research phase)
  • Conversion: 1-3%
  • Commission: Multiple affiliate links

Tutorial Articles (25%)

Example: "How to Write Blog Posts 10x Faster with AI"

  • Target: People learning to use tools
  • Intent: Low-Medium (trust building)
  • Conversion: 0.5-2%
  • Commission: Soft sell, but high volume potential

Keyword Research System

I don't guess what to write. I use data.

My Criteria

  • Search volume: 500-10,000 monthly searches
  • Difficulty: Low (can rank in 3-6 months)
  • Intent: Commercial (buying intent)
  • Commission potential: $25+ per sale

Tools I Use

  • Free: Google Keyword Planner, AnswerThePublic
  • Paid: Ahrefs, Semrush (when profitable)
  • Competitor analysis: What's ranking, what's missing

Content Production Pipeline

This is where the automation happens.

Step 1: Research (30 min)

  • Identify target keyword
  • Analyze top 5 ranking articles
  • Find gaps (what they're missing)
  • Plan article structure

Step 2: AI Writing (15 min)

I use Claude Code with this prompt:

Write a 2,500-word SEO article about [topic].

Requirements:
- Target keyword: [primary keyword]
- Secondary keywords: [list]
- Structure: H1, 5 H2s, 2-3 H3s per H2
- Include: Introduction, features, pricing, pros/cons, FAQ
- Tone: Professional but conversational
- Add affiliate CTAs naturally
- Include comparison tables where relevant

Step 3: Optimization (30 min)

  • Add images from Unsplash
  • Insert affiliate links with tracking IDs
  • Add internal links to related articles
  • Write meta title and description
  • Check mobile responsiveness

Step 4: Publishing (5 min)

  • Git add, commit, push
  • Netlify auto-deploys
  • Submit to Google Search Console
  • Share on social media

Common Mistakes

What Not to Do

  • Promote garbage products: Kills trust, hurts conversions
  • Keyword stuffing: Google penalizes, readers hate it
  • Ignore mobile: 60%+ of traffic is mobile
  • No email list: You're building on rented land
  • Give up too early: SEO takes 3-6 months to work
